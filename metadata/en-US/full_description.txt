Ad-Free is an ad blocker for Spotify.
You can pick from one of three options to play instead of the ad:

1. Mute audio
2. Local music files
3. Interdimensional Cable from Rick and Morty

Features:

* Mute audio when advertisement is playing
* Play arbitrary audio instead of Spotify advertisement
* No ROOT required
* Privacy focused, we do not spy on you
* Plugin based design, easily extend it for more support.


Browse https://adfree.abertschi.ch for more information.
